Privacy Policy

RenovateIQ Business · Last updated: May 2026

This policy applies to RenovateIQ Business only. For the RenovateIQ consumer app, see renovateiq.co.uk/privacy.

Overview

RenovateIQ Business is a professional renovation management app connecting contractors, clients and subcontractors. This privacy policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Name — used to identify you within projects
  • Email address — used for account creation and verification
  • Photos — uploaded by contractors as project progress updates
  • Messages — sent between contractors, clients and subcontractors within the app
  • User ID — generated to link your account to your projects

How We Use Your Data

All data is used solely to provide the app's functionality. We do not sell, share or use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, hosted in Europe (London). Firebase is compliant with GDPR.

Third Party Services

  • Google Firebase — authentication and data storage
  • RevenueCat — subscription management for contractor accounts
  • Apple — payment processing for subscriptions

Subscriptions

Contractor subscriptions are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not store payment card details.

Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. To request deletion contact: joannagillan@outlook.com

Children's Privacy

RenovateIQ Business is intended for professional use and is not directed at children under 13.

Contact

For any privacy related questions contact: joannagillan@outlook.com

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
