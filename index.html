FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about RenovateIQ and home renovation planning.

Is RenovateIQ free?
RenovateIQ is free to download with no account or login required. The free version includes 1 project, task checklists, budget tracking, photo gallery and weekly notifications. A one-time Pro upgrade (£3.99) unlocks unlimited projects, contractor notes, timeline view, PDF export and share progress features.
What devices does RenovateIQ work on?

Do I need an account to use RenovateIQ?
No account or login is required. Just download the app and start planning your renovation straight away.
How do I plan a home renovation?
Start by defining your scope and setting a realistic budget — including a 15-20% contingency for unexpected costs. Break the project into phases: preparation, build and finishing. Get at least three quotes from contractors, agree a payment schedule and track every expense as you go. RenovateIQ is designed to help you manage all of this in one place.
How do I track my renovation budget?
Set your total budget before work begins, then log every expense as it happens — contractor invoices, materials, fixtures and delivery charges. Categorise your spending so you can see exactly where the money is going and compare it against your original estimates. RenovateIQ's budget tracker does all of this automatically.
How do I find reliable tradespeople?
Always get a minimum of three quotes and ask for references from recent similar projects. Check that tradespeople are registered with the relevant bodies — Gas Safe for gas work, NICEIC or NAPIT for electrical work. Review their ratings on Checkatrade or Trustpilot and never pay the full amount upfront. RenovateIQ lets you store all your contractor details in one place so nothing gets lost.
